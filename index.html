About

Born in Guangxi, China. PhD candidate in Electronic Engineering at CUHK, supervised by Prof. Jianbin Xu. My research focuses on thermal transport, machine-learning interatomic potentials, and data-driven methodologies for functional materials. I actively work on open-source tools for atomistic simulations and reproducible workflows.

Research Interests

Thermal transport and advanced computational methodologies Molecular dynamics and DFT calculations of phononic properties Machine-learning interatomic potentials and foundational models AI for Science in multi-functional material systems
