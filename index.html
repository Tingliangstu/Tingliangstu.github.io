About

Born in Guangxi, China. Doctoral researcher in Electronic Engineering at CUHK, supervised by Prof. Jianbin Xu. My work combines molecular dynamics, DFT-informed modeling, and AI4Science methods to understand and control phonon transport in low-dimensional and complex material systems.

Research Interests

Thermal transport and advanced computational methodologies Molecular dynamics and DFT calculations of phononic properties Machine-learning interatomic potentials and foundation models AI for Science in multifunctional material systems
